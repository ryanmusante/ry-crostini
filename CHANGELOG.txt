4.10.5  2026-03-24

  Correctness:
  - change vm.swappiness from 10 to 60 in 99-crostini-memory.conf;
    aggressive anti-swap on 4 GB with no dedicated swap starves page
    reclaim and triggers OOM-killer on container lifecycle daemons
  - change vm.overcommit_memory from 1 to 0 in 99-crostini-tuning.conf;
    mode 1 (always overcommit) on 4 GB RAM with no swap leads to OOM-
    killer killing garcon/sommelier on container boot
  - add -e flag to crostini-sysctl.service ExecStart; suppresses
    "Invalid argument" / "Read-only file system" errors from container-
    unsupported keys (net.ipv4.ping_group_range, kernel.unprivileged_
    userns_clone) that caused systemd to log the service as failed
  - add upgrade paths for all three changes with $ anchors to prevent
    matching unrelated values (e.g. vm.swappiness=100)
